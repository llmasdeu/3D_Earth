Considerations:

The camera position can be moved with:

- Mouse (click & drag to the sides).
- Keyboard (W to get closer to the Sun, S to move away, A to move to the right, D to move to the left).
- While there aren’t any keys pressed, the camera will move automatically.

In addition to that, we’ve added two more functions with keys:

- While pressed the F key, the camera will be in a position where the normals texture of the Earth can be seen properly.
- While pressed the G key, the camera will be in a position where the nocturnal texture of the Earth can be seen properly.

Finally, we’ve also escalated and added automatic rotation to all the planets (in both directions, and at different velocities). We’ve added a second light to Venus (from Mars), as well.
